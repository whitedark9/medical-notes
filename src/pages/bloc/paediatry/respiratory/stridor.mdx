# Stridor

> Describe differential diagnosis for a 2 year old child presenting with high fever and stridor. Discuss the management of one cause. (UMMG)

> Discuss about the common differential diagnosis of acute stridor and describe the investigations and management of each differential diagnosis. (UMTG)

## Definition

> Stridor is a nosiy and difficult breathing due to partial obstruction of upper airway

## Common Differential Diagnosis of Acute Stridor

### 1. Acute laryngotracheo-bronchitis

- **Onset**: 1-2 days
- Fever (+)
- Coryza
- Barking cough
- Hoarseness
- Tachypnoea
- Respiratory difficulty

### 2. Acute epiglottitis

- **Onset**: hours
- High fever
- Drooling of saliva
- Toxic
- Sits leaning forward
- Neck extended
- Cherry red epiglottis

### 3. Laryngeal diphtheria

- Fever (+)
- DPT immunization status (-)
- Toxic
- Bull neck
- Grayish-white membrane on throat examination

### 4. Angioneurotic edema

- Sudden onset
- History of allergy
- Non-pitting edema on limbs
- Urticarial rash

### 5. Retropharyngeal abscess

- Bulging post pharyngeal wall
- Hyperextension of the neck

### 6. Foreign body

- Sudden onset
- History of choking
- Unequal air entry
- Reduce air entry
- Cyanosis (+)
- Fever (-)

### Specific Management

#### 1. Epiglottitis

#### 2. Foreign Body

- For infant, hold the child upside down and slap on the back
- Heimlich maneuver
- Oxygen
- Tracheostomy
- Bronchoscopic removal

#### 3. Diphtheria

- `IM Benzyl penicillin 0.5 L/kg/dose 6 hourly for 7-10 days` **Or** `Erythromycin 50mg/kg/day 6 hourly for 10 days` (in penicillin allergy) **Plus**
- `IV/IM diphtheria antitoxin 40,000 immunization`
- Tracheostomy

#### 4. Acute Laryngotracheo Bronchitis

- Hydration
- Close monitoring
- `Dexamethasone IM or oral 0.3 mg/kg/dose OD`, maximum 3 days depending on response
- If severe, intubation and ventilation

#### 5. Retropharyngeal Abscess

- `Antibiotic`: `Inj C.pen 50,000 U/kg 6hourly + Inj. Cloxacillin 25 mg/kg 6hourly`
- Refer for incision

#### 6. Angioneurotic Edema

- `S/C adrenaline: 1:1000` 0.5ml in older, 0.25ml in younger children
- `IM chlorpheniramine 5-10 mg`
- `IV hydrocortisone 5 mg/kg/dose`
