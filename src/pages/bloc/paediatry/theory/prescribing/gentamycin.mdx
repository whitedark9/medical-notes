# Gentamycin

> Gentamycin (UM2)

### Route & Dose

- IM or slow IV
- Newborn < 32 week: `5 mg/kg 36 hr`
- Newborn > 32 week: `5 mg/kg 24 hr`
- Child: `2.5 mg/kg 8 hr`

### Contraindication

- ~~Myasthemia gravis~~

### Cautions

- Pregnancy
- Auditory & Vestibular damage in fetus
- Renal impairment
- Infant and elderly
- Avoid prolong use
- Condition with muscular weakness

### Side Effect

- Hypersensitivity
- Ototoxicity
- Nephrotoxicity (usually reversible)
- Impairment of neuromuscular transmission
- Antibiotic associated colitis, nausea, vomiting, rash

### Warnings

- Tow aminoglycosides must never be given simultaneously
- To prevent ototoxicity, concurrent use with diuretics is avoided if possible
