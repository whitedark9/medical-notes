# Acute Diarrhoea

> Take history from \_\_\_ year old child presenting with loose motion.

## History

import { Steps } from "nextra/components";

<Steps>

### Introduction

- Introduce yourself
- Explain and get permission

### History of Present Illness

1. `Loose motion`
   - Duration
   - Frequency
   - Character of stool (_watery, bloody, mucous_)
   - Colour of stool (_Yellowish, rice water_)
1. `Associated Symptomes`
   - **Vomiting**
     - Duration
     - Frequency
     - Character of vomitus
   - **Abdominal pain**
     - Duration
     - Site
   - **Fever**
     - Onset (_sudden, gradual_)
     - Duration
     - Pattern (_continuous, intermittent, remittent_)
     - Temperature (_low grade, high grade_)
     - Associated symptoms:
       - chills & rigor
       - evening rise in temperature
       - sweating
       - Medication (_sponge taping, paracetamol_)
1. `Severity/Dehydration Assessment`
   - Thirsty (_drink normally or eagerly_)
   - Drowsiness or restlessness
   - Sunken eyes
1. `Features of Complications`
   - Cold & clammy extremities (_sign of shock_)
   - Oliguria (_sign of shock_)
   - Floppiness (_hypokalaemia_)
   - Fits (_hyponatremia_)
   - Depressed anterior fontanellae (_in young infants_)

### Past Medical History

1. `Similar attack` & Frequency
1. History of `hospitalization`, frequency, its reason
1. Retro infection
1. Lactose-intolerance
1. Worm infestation

### Immunization History

- Rota, Measles

### Social History

- Water supply
- Safe drinking Water
- Latrine/Sewage disposal
- Personal hygiene and hand washing
- Mother's education
- Income

### Family History

- Similar illness in family members

### Developmental History

- Normal weight gain or development of the child (_PEM_)

### Feeding History

- Exclusive breast feeding
- Breast or bottle feeding
- Food preparation
- Hand washing, Bottle washing
- Caretaker

</Steps>

## Physical Examination

<Steps>

### Introduction [#PE]

- Introduce yourself
- Explain and get permission

### General Inspection

- Well or ill
- Alert or drowsy or lethargy (_dehydration assessment_)
- Nutritional status (_PEM_)

### Head

- Febrile or afebrile
- Anterior fontanellae (_depressed_)
- Sunken eyes
- Eyes (_glistening, dry_)
- Mouth & Tongue (_moist, dry_)
- Thirst (_drink normally, eagerly, poorly_)

### Extremities

- Cold & clammy extremities or warm (_sign of shock_)
- Pulse - Volume (_weak, good_)
- Blood pressure

### Abdomen

1. `Inspection`

   - Shape (_distension_)

1. `Palpation`

   - Skin pinch (_go back quicky, slowly or very slowly_)
   - Liver
   - Spleen

1. `Auscultation`
   - Bowel sounds (_increase or decrease in hypokalaemia_)

### Chest

- Respiratory rate [^1] (_acidotic breathing_)

[^1]: **Fast breathing**: 0-2 months (>60/min), 2-12 months (>50/min), 1-5 years (>40/min)

</Steps>
