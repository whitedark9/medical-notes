# Acute Respiratory Infection

> Take history from \_\_\_ year old child presenting with recent onset of cough with difficult breathing.

## History

import { Steps } from "nextra/components";

<Steps>

### Introduction

- Introduce yourself
- Explain and get permission

### History of Present Illness

1. `Cough`
   - Duration
   - Dry or wet cough
   - Spasmodic in nature (_wooping cough_)
1. `Fever`
   - Onset (_sudden, gradual_)
   - Duration
   - Pattern (_continuous, intermittent, remittent_)
   - Temperature (_low grade, high grade_)
   - Associated symptoms:
     - chills & rigor
     - evening rise in temperature
     - sweating
   - Medication (_sponge taping, paracetamol_)
1. `Severity Assessment`
   - **Nosiy breathing**
     - Stridor
     - Grunting
     - Wheezing (_asthma_)
   - **Fast breathing**
   - **Chest indrawing**
1. `Danger Signs` (မှိန်း, ပြာ, တက်, နို့မစို့, အန်, ညည်း)
   - Drowsiness, sleepy
   - Bluish coloration of lip and finger tip
   - Convulsions
   - Poor feeding
   - Vomiting
   - Grunding

### Past Medical History

1. `Similar attack`
1. `Repeated chest infection`
1. History of `hospitalization`, frequency, its reason
1. History of `TB` or `TB contact`

### Immunization History

- DPT, BCG, Polio, Measles

### Feeding History

- Exclusive breast feeding
- Breast or bottle feeding

### Family History

- Similar illness in the family
- Overcrowded
- Ventilation and housing condition
- Smoking, indoor & outdoor air pollution
- TB contact

</Steps>

## Physical Examination

<Steps>

### Introduction [#PE]

- Introduce yourself
- Explain and get permission

### General Inspection

- Well or ill
- Alert or drowsy
- Nutritional status
- Dyspnoeic

### Head

- Febrile or afebrile
- **Nose**: Alar nasi, Nasal polyps
- **Tongue**: Central cyanosis, Tonsilitis
- **Noisy breathing**: Stridor, Wheezing, Grunting

### Extremities

- Clubbing (_suppurative lung disease_)
- Peripheral cyanosis
- Warm hands/palms (_respiratory failure_)
- Pulse
  - Volume (_bounding pulse in respiratory failure_)

### Chest

#### Inspection (Anterior)

- Shape (_Pectus excavatum, Pectus Carinatum_)
- Scar, Chest tube
- Movement of chest
- Respiratory rate[^1] (_fast breathing_)
- Chest indrawing

[^1]: **Fast breathing**: 0-2 months (>60/min), 2-12 months (>50/min), 1-5 years (>40/min)

#### Palpation (Anterior)

- Trachea position
- Movement of chest
- Apex beat position
- Vocal fremitus

#### Percussion (Anterior)

#### Auscultation (Anterior)

- Breath sound
- Added sound (_rhonchi, crepitations_)
- Vocal resonance

#### Inspection (Posterior)

- Shape (_Pectus excavatum, Pectus Carinatum_)
- Scar, Chest tube
- Spine deformity

#### Palpation (Posterior)

- Vocal fremitus

#### Percussion (Posterior)

#### Auscultation (Posterior)

- Breath sound
- Added sound (_rhonchi, crepitations_)
- Vocal resonance

</Steps>

## Differential Diagnosis

1. Severe pneumonia/Very severe pneumonia
1. Severe bronchiolitis
1. Acute bronchial asthma
1. Viral induced wheeze
