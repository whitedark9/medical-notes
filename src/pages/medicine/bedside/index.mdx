# Introduction

## History Format

0. Introduction
1. Particulars of the Patient
1. Chief Complaints
1. History of Present Illness (HOPI)
1. System Review
1. Past Medical History
1. Past Surgical History
1. Personal History
1. Family History
1. Social History
1. Obstetrics and Gynaecological History [Female]
1. ICE

### 1. Particulars of the Patient

1. Name
1. Age
1. Sex
1. Marital status (_single/married/divorced/widow or widower_)
1. Address
1. Date of admission
1. Hopital/Ward Unit
1. Race, Religion

### 2. Cheif Complaints

> Do not use medical terms!!!

> Must be in chronological order by duration.

### 3. History of Present Illness

> Symptom analysis/Analysis of chief complaints

1. System/Symptom Analysis
1. Differential Diagnosis
1. Precipitating factors
1. Complication
1. On admission (_current medications, tests_)
1. [Background history] (_e.g., referred from other hospital_)

1. **S**ite

1. **A**ssociated symptoms
1. **T**iming
1. **E**xacerbating factors and relieving factors
1. **S**everity

### 4. System Review

> Can use medical terms.

#### Cardiovascular System

1. Chest pain/Chest discomfort
1. Dyspnoea (Breathlessness)
1. Palpitation
1. Syncope/Presyncope
1. Oedema

#### Respiratory System

1. Cough
1. Sputum
1. Haemoptysis
1. Chest pain/Chest discomfort
1. Dyspnoea
1. Wheeze

#### Gastro-intestinal System

1. General
   - Loss of weight & loss of appetite (anorexia)
   - Abdominal pain & distension
1. Upper GI
   - Painful lips, tongue & mouth
   - Dysphagia (difficulty in swallowing)
   - Odynophagia (pain on swallowing)
   - Heartburn & Reflux symptoms
   - Dyspepsis (Pain or discomfort in upper abdomen)
   - Nausea & Vomiting
   - Haematemesis
   - elaena
   - Wind (Belching)
1. Lower GI
   - Wind (Excessive/offensive flatus or borborygmi)
   - Altered bowel habit (constipation, diarrhoea, steatorrhoea)
   - Hematochezia (rectal bleeding)
1. Hepatobiliary
   - Jaundice
   - Pruritus (Itchiness)

#### Genitourinary System

1. Dysuria (pain on passing micturition)
1. Frequency
1. Urgency
1. Strangury (force to empty bladder)
1. nocturia
1. Haematuria
1. Polyuria
1. Oliguria
1. Anuria

#### Central Nervous System

1. Headache
1. Syncope
1. Epileptic seizures
1. Dizziness
1. Fall
1. Visual disturbance (_double vision, blurred vision_)
1. Ocular pain

### 5. Past Medical History

1. Similar attack
1. History of hospitalization
1. Non-communicable diseases (_DM, HT, IHD_)
1. Communicable diseases (_TB, HIV_)
1. Blood transfusion
1. [Travelling history]

### 6. Past Surgery History

- Minor/Major operation

### 7. Drug History

1. Regular/Prolong use of drugs
1. Current medications
1. Previous medications
1. Drug allergy

### 8. Personal History

1. Smoking (_Pack yers = Number of cigarettes smoked per day/20 x Number of years smoking_)
1. Betel chewing
1. Alcohol
1. Education status
1. Diet
1. Lifestyle (_physical activity_)

### 9. Social History

1. Housing
1. Lighting, ventilation, overcrowding
1. Sanitary condition, water supply
1. Surrounding environment
1. [Pets]

### 10. Family History

1. Parents, siblings, children
1. Similar attack/disease

### 11. Obstetrics & Gynaecological History [Female]

1. Menarche
   - Regular/Irregular
   - Duration
   - Dysmenorrhoea
1. Menopause

### 12. ICE

1. Idea of Patient (အခုရောဂါက ဘာ့ကြောင့် ဖြစ်ရတာလို့ ထင်သလဲ။ ဘာရောဂါ ဖြစ်တယ်လို့ သံသယရှိခဲ့သလဲ။ အခုရောဂါနဲ့ ပတ်သက်ပြီး ဘာတွေ သိထားသလဲ။)
1. Concern of Patient (အခုရောဂါဖြစ်တာနဲ့ ပတ်သက်ပြီး ဘာတွေ စိုးရိမ်မိတာ ရှိသလဲ။)
1. Expectation of Patient (ရေရှည်မှာ ဒီရောဂါကို ဘယ်လို ထိန်းသွားမယ်လို့ စိတ်ကူးထားသလဲ။ ရောဂါပျောက်သွားရင် ဘာတွေ ဆောင်မယ်၊ ရှောင်မယ်လို့ တွေးထားသလဲ။ ဒီရောဂါက နောင်အခါမှာ ကိုယ့်ရဲ့ နေ့စဥ်ဘဝကို ဘယ်လိုခက်ခဲလာစေနိုင်တယ်လို့ မြင်မိသလဲ။)
