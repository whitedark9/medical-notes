# GI Bleeding

## Causes of Upper GI Bleeding (acute)

1. Oeophagitis (10%): Usually with hiatus hernia
1. Varices (2-9%): Liver disease, Portal vein thrombosis
1. Mallory-Weiss tear (5%): Retching
1. Peptic ulcer (35-50%): NSAIDs, _H.Pylori_
1. Cancer of stomach or oesophagus (2%)
1. Gastric erosions (10-20%): NSAIDs, Alcohol
1. Vascular malformations (5%)
1. Aorto-duodenal fistula (0.2%): Aortic graft

## Causes of Lower GI Bleeding

### Severe Acute

> DAMII

- `D`iverticular disease
- `A`ngiodysplasia
- `M`eckel's diverticulum
- `I`schaemia
- `I`nflammatory bowel disease (rare)

### Moderate, Chronic/Subacute

> Poly-FAR RICH

- Large `poly`ps
- `F`issure
- `A`ngiodysplasia
- `R`adiation enteritis
- Solitary `r`ectal ulcer
- `I`nflammatory bowel disease
- `C`arcinoma
- `H`aemorrhoids
