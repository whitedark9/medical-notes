# Photo 04

![Photo](/surgery/photo/04.jpeg)

A 38-year-old lady presents with breast lump for 8 months.

<details>
<summary>What is the clinical sign of the picture?</summary>

Peau de orange

</details>

<details>
<summary>Explain the cause of this clinical sign.</summary>

Dermal lymphoedema due to blockage of dermal lymphatics by carcinomatous cells

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the clinical diagnosis?</summary>

Advanced carcinoma of right breast

</details>

<details>
<summary>How would you confirm the diagnosis in an outpatient clinic?</summary>

- Fine needle aspiration cytology (FNAC)

</details>
