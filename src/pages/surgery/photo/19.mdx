This baby has the operation done 2 days after birth.

<details>
<summary>What is the reddish thing on her abdomen?</summary>

Temporary transverse loop colostomy

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the points for diagnosis?</summary>

Mucosa lined viscus in the anterior abdominal wall, which shows two openings

</details>

<details>
<summary>What complication do you noticed?</summary>

Excoriation of the surrounding skin

</details>

<details>
<summary>For which disease had this operation performed.</summary>

Hirschsprung's disease

</details>
