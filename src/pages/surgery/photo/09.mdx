# Photo 09

![Photo](/surgery/photo/09.jpeg)

This lady has the condition as shown in the neck but without other associated features.

<details>
<summary>What is the condition?</summary>

Solitary thyroid nodule

</details>

<details>
<summary>What 3 clinical features that you would like to do to help you decide the treatment?</summary>

1. Features of thyrotoxicosis
1. Features of retrosternal prolongation
1. Features of malignancy

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is treatment?</summary>

- Aspiration to find out cystic or solid
- Cystic:
  - Closely followed up
  - If recurs, repeat aspiration.
  - If recurs again, `Hemi-thyroidectomy`
- Solid: `Hemi-thyroidectomy`

</details>
