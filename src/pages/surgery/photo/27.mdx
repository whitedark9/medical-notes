This patient has a painless swelling on the right wrist for 6 months

<details>
<summary>What is the diagnosis?</summary>

Dorsal ganglion of the wrist and ankle

</details>

<details>
<summary>How would you confirm the diagnosis?</summary>

On inspection,

- Hemispherical swelling over the joint.
- When the patient clenched the hand and flexed the wrist, swelling becoming more prominent and immobile

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name two methods of treatment.</summary>

1. Surgical excision
1. Intra-lesional corticosteroid injection

</details>
