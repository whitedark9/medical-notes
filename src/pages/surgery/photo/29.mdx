# Photo 29

![Photo](/surgery/photo/29.jpeg)

This baby has the swelling in the occipital area since birth

<details>
<summary>What is the clinical diagnosis?</summary>

Occipital, nasal or sacral meningocele

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name one other disease?</summary>

Meningomyelocele/meningoencephalocele

</details>

<details>
<summary>How will you differentiate the two?</summary>

`Transillumination test`

- Meningocele is brilliantly translucent
- Meningoencephalocele are not

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are you going to do?</summary>

Refer the patient to neurosurgeon for further management

</details>
