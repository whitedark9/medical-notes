This 35 year old male had a painless swelling for 3 years which grows slowly

<details>
<summary>What is the clinical diagnosis?</summary>

Sebaceous cyst scalp

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the points for the diagnosis?</summary>

Swelling hemispherical in shape over the scalp where sebaceous glands are numerous. (punctum present insome cases)

</details>

<details>
<summary>What will happen if you do a pinch test?</summary>

Cannot pinch the skin over the swelling

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the treatment?</summary>

Excision under local anaesthesia

</details>
