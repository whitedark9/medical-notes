This is the external genitalia of a 27-year-old man.

<details>
<summary>What is the clinical diagnosis?</summary>

Carcinoma of penis

</details>

<details>
<summary>How are you going to confirm the diagnosis?</summary>

`Wedge biopsy` under local block

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the treatment?</summary>

- `Partial amputation` under spinal anaesthesia
- Antibiotics & Regular follow-up
- Check glands in the inguinal area disappear or not
- If persists or growing, perform inguinal dissection in one side and ten in the other side

</details>
