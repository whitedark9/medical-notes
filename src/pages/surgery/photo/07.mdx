This is right foot of 43 year old diabetic patient.

<details>
<summary>Which structure seen in the floor of the ulcer? (Figure 2)</summary>

Tendon of the extensor hallucis longus and slough

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the diagnosis?</summary>

Diabetic gangrene and ulceration

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the points for diagnosis</summary>

- 43 year old diabetic patient
- Ulcer on the dorsum of the right foot which has oedematous and inflamed surrounding margin and patches of darkened areas

</details>

<details>
<summary>Management</summary>

1. Control of diabetes with insulin
1. Anti-biotics according to culture and sensitivity
1. Wound debridement and daily dressing with anti-septic solution

</details>
