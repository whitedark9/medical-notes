# Photo 08

![Photo](/surgery/photo/08.jpeg)

The patient is a 35 year old lady.

<details>
<summary>What is your clinical diagnosis?</summary>

Grave's disease, Primary thyrotoxicosis

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the findings?</summary>

1. Thyroid enlargement
1. Presence of eye signs (e.g. proptosis, exophthalmos, lid retraction and stare)

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the investigations to confirm the diagnosis?</summary>

- Serum T3, T4 and TSH

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name 3 treatment options?</summary>

1. Medical treatment
1. Subtotal thyroidectomy
1. Radio-iodine therapy

</details>
