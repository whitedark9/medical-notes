# Photo 26

![Photo](/surgery/photo/26.jpeg)

This is the picture of face of 71-year-old white man who presents with chronic ulcer for 3 years.

<details>
<summary>What is your diagnosis?</summary>

Basal cell carcinoma of the face

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the diagnostic features?</summary>

1. 71-year-old white man
1. Chronic ulcer on the face for 3 years
1. Ulcer has raised and rolled up edge, irregular floor covered by blackish necrotic slough

</details>

<details>
<summary>How would you confirm the diagnosis?</summary>

`Wedge biopsy` of the ulcer margin

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name 2 differential diagnoses?</summary>

1. Basal cell carcinoma
1. Malignant melanoma

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name 2 methods of treatment.</summary>

1. Wide excision and skin grafting
1. Radiotherapy

</details>
