A doctor is performing a clinical test.

<details>
<summary>What is the test?</summary>

Can get above the swelling

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the value of this test?</summary>

To differentiate inguino-scrotal from scrotal swelling

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the rationale of this test?</summary>

One cannot get above the swelling because inguinoscrotal swelling descends from the inguinal canal

</details>

<details>
<summary>How would you treat this patient?</summary>

Herniotomy and Herniorrhaphy

</details>
