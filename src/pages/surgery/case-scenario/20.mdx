# Case 20

> A 75 year old lady just slipped and cannot rise up by herself. She complained of pain around her left hip.

<details>
<summary>Which fracture is the most likely?</summary>

Fracture neck of femur

</details>

<details>
<summary>Any differentials.</summary>

Trochanteric fracture of femur

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the most common underlying risk factor at this age for easy-fractures?</summary>

Osteoporosis

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the risks of being bed-ridden by this sort of injury?</summary>

- Pulmonary embolism
- Deep vein thrombosis
- Bed sore
- Hypostatic pneumonia

</details>
