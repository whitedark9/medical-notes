# Case 26

> A 30 year old lady came to ERC for acute agitated state. Examination revealed a soft vascular thyroid and tachycardia.

<details>
<summary>What is your diagnosis?</summary>

Diffuse toxic goiter (Grave's disease)

</details>

<details>
<summary>What other signs would you like to look for?</summary>

1. Eye signs: Exophthalmos, Lid lag, Lid retraction
1. Pretibial myxoedema
1. Proximal myopathy

</details>

<details>
<summary>Give confirmatory lab test for your diagnosis.</summary>

Thyroid function test
- T3 and T4 - increase
- TSH - decrease

</details>

<details>
<summary>Mention treatment options for your diagnosis.</summary>

1. Anti-thyroid drugs: `Carbimazole` or `Propylthiouracil`
1. Radio-iodine therapy
1. Surgery: `Subtotal` or `near total thyroidectomy`

</details>
