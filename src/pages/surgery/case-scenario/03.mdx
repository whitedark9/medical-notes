# Case 03

> A 65 year old man came to the surgical OPD for inability to pass urine for 12 hours. On examination, SPA was distended with sense of micturition.

<details>
<summary>What is the situation called?</summary>

Acute retention of urine (AROU)

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is your initial line of treatment?</summary>

Urinary catheterization

</details>

<details>
<summary>Give 2 main risks of this situation.</summary>

1. Bladder rupture
1. Bladder atony

</details>

<details>
<summary>Give any 2 possible causes for this.</summary>

1. Benign prostatic hypertrophy (BPH)
1. Ca Prostate

</details>
