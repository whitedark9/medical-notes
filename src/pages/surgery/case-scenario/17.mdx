# Case 17

> A teenager girl is anxious for recent awareness of a lump in her right breast. The lump is well discrete and freely mobile with no other positive findings.

<details>
<summary>What is the most possible diagnosis?</summary>

Fibroadenoma breast (Right side)

</details>

<details>
<summary>What investigations would you offer?</summary>

Triple Assessment

1. Clinical assessment: History, Physical examination
1. Imaging: Ultrasound breast, Mammogram
1. Histopathology: Fine needle aspiration biopsy (FNAB), Core needle biopsy

</details>

<details>
<summary>Any differentials? (Any two)</summary>

1. Fibrocystic disease
1. Breast cancer

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name one surgical treatment for your diagnosis.</summary>

1. Excision and Biopsy

</details>
