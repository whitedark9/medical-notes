# Case 21

> A 35 year old man rushed to ERC for sudden unbearable pain along right leg. Physical findings are marked pallor of sole and toe pads of right leg with motor weakness.

<details>
<summary>What is the initial diagnosis?</summary>

Acute limb ischemia of right leg

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the possible causes for this? (Any 3)</summary>

1. Thrombosis
1. Embolism
1. Aneurysm

</details>

<details>
<summary>Give your urgent management plan.</summary>

- Hospital admission
- Resuscitation
- IV `heparin`
- Urgent angiogram
- Urgent intervention: `Embolectomy` and `Thrombolysis` with tissue plasminogen activator (tPA)

</details>
