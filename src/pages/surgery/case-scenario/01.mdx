# Case 01

> A 23 year old boy presented to the ERC with a distended abdomen and acute vomiting. Examination revealed a tense swelling at right groin above the groin skin crease.

<details>
<summary>What is your provisional diagnosis?</summary>

Obstructed right inguinal hernia

</details>

<details>
<summary>Give initial management for this boy.</summary>

Resuscitation

- Assess vital signs
- NPO (nothing by mouth) & Ryle tube suction
- IV broad spectrum antibiotics and IV analgesic
- Give oxygen
- Assess IV line with 2 wide bore canulae
- Give IV fluid

</details>

<details>
<summary>Name surgical treatment for your diagnosis.</summary>

Herniotomy and herniorrhaphy

</details>
