# Case 12

> Following RTA, a young lady was found to be dyspnoeic with hyper-resonant right lung field. Examination also noted a pradox movement in mid-right chest.

<details>
<summary>What is the diagnosis?</summary>

Flail chest (Right side)

</details>

<details>
<summary>How would you reduce tension in pleural space under critical condition?</summary>

Needle thoracocenthesis (2nd ICS mid-clavicular line)

</details>

<details>
<summary>Outline your management for this lady.</summary>

Needle thoracostomy followed by

- ICD tube (Intercostal drainage tube) with underwater-sealed drainage system
- ICU & HDU care (Intensive care unit & High density unit care)
- Ventilatory support if respiratory failure suspected

</details>
