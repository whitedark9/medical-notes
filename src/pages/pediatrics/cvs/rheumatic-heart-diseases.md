# Rheumatic Heart Diseases

## Acute Carditis

1. **Endocarditis**
   - One or more cardiac murmurs, such as PSM and MDM
1. **Myocarditis**
   - Cardiomegaly
   - Soft first heart sound
   - Tachycardia
   - Gallop rhythm [^1]
   - Congestive heart failure
