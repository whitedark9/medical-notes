### Route & Dose

- Oral
- `20 mg/kg sulfamethoxazole` & `4 mg/kg trimethoprim BD`

### Contraindication

- ~~Pregnancy~~
- ~~Infant < 6 weeks (especially with neonatal jaundice/preterm)~~
- ~~Liver disease~~
- ~~Blood disorder~~

### Cautions

- Renal impairment
- Breast feeding mother
- Check blood count
- Increased fluid intake
- G6PD deficiency
- Asthma

### Side Effect

- Steven Johnson's syndrome
- Bone marrow depression
- Allergy
- GI disturbance
- Antibiotic associated colitis
