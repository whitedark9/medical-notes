# Cefotaxime

> Cefotaxime (UM2)

### Indication

import { Callout } from "nextra/components";

<Callout emoji={""}>

- _Streptococci_
- _Staph. aureus_
- Gram (-)ve bacteria (particularly penicillin resistant _pneumococci_ and _H. influenzae_)
- Meningitis meningococal septicaemia
- Severe sepsis
- Epiglottitis
- RTI
- UTI
- Soft tissue infection

</Callout>

### Route & Dose

- Neonate \<= 7 days: `50 mg/kg/dose 12 hourly`
- Neonate > 7 days: `50 mg/kg/dose 6-8 hourly`
- Child: `50 mg/kg/dose 12 hourly` (6 hourly in meningitis & other severe sepsis)

### Contraindication

- ~~Known or suspected allergy to cephalosporins/penicillin~~

### Side Effect

- Hypersensitivity
- Antibiotic associated colitis, nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea
